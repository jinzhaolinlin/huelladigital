Digital Footprint

Define the term: Digital Footprint?

The digital footprint is the trace you leave when browsing the Internet. Every time you click or like on social networks, or when you use an application from your cell phone or computer, you leave personal information.

Tips for taking care of your digital footprint?

1.-Be careful with social networks 2.-Use multiple browsers 3.-Create strong passwords

Drawbacks?

1.-Your personal information could be compromised as part of a data breach and fraudulent use of data. 2.-Cybercriminals can take advantage of your digital footprint, using it for purposes such as phishing to access your account or creating false identities based on your data.

Responsibilities as a student?

take care of my fingerprint keep my fingerprint secure,use appropriate,do not enter strange applications

Recommendations / conclusions?

1.-Use strong passwords. 2.-Better biometric data (fingerprint, face, iris) to better protect the information you store. 3.-Update your device regularly.
